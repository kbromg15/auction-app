User Guide

Welcome to the Your Sales page! Here are some tips to help you:

  • Review the list of items you have sold.
  • Click on the item title to view more details about the auction.
  • The Amount column shows the final sale price of the item.
  • To view the auction details, click the View button.
  • To remove a sale, click the Remove button and confirm the action.
  • To return to the home page, click the Back to Home button.
