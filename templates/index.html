User Guide

Welcome to the Auction App! Here are some quick tips to get you started:

  • If you are a new user, click on the Register button to create an account.
  • If you already have an account, click on the Login button to access your account.
  • Once logged in, you can add items for auction by clicking on the Add Item button.
  • To view all available auctions, click on the View Auctions button.
  • You can view your bids, sales, and watchlist by clicking on the respective buttons.
  • To log out, click on the Logout button.
